## About

*Astrosynthesis* develops a substrate-first theory of physical emergence built on the **Collapse Tension Substrate (CTS)** — a pre-geometric scalar field whose excitations survive, persist, or decay according to quantifiable structural criteria.

The book derives from first principles:
- Why structures appear at all
- What determines whether they survive
- How geometry, time, matter, and stability emerge from persistence dynamics
